# Sangam brand identity

![Sangam primary mark](../frontend/public/sangam-mark.svg)

The Sangam mark represents two equal currents forming one document: a human and an identified agent working through the same document API. The document remains negative space so the idea survives without labels or extra symbols.

## Primary mark

Use [`sangam-mark.svg`](../frontend/public/sangam-mark.svg) for normal product, documentation, and repository branding.

- Ink: `#102A43`
- Cobalt: `#2F80ED`
- Preferred light field: `#EDF4FB` or white

The two colors always receive equal visual weight. Do not add gradients, shadows, glow, or theme-specific recoloring.

## Monochrome mark

Use [`sangam-mark-monochrome.svg`](../frontend/public/sangam-mark-monochrome.svg) when only one color is available or when the primary colors do not have sufficient contrast.

- Forest ink: `#20241F`
- Preferred light field: `#FFF8E8` or white

On a dark field, the monochrome path may be rendered in white, but its geometry must not change.

## Usage rules

- Preserve clear space equal to at least one quarter of the mark's width.
- Do not place the primary mark directly on a dark or visually busy background; use a light tile or the reversed monochrome mark.
- Do not separate, rotate, or animate the two currents independently.
- Do not add a cloud, sparkle, database, server, infinity symbol, or other explanatory icon.
- Use the word `Sangam` as live text beside the mark in the interface rather than baking a wordmark into the SVG.
